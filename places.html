My Favourite Places

Explore the places I love to visit

Tokyo, Japan

The vibrant heart of Japan, Tokyo offers a fascinating mix of traditional and futuristic experiences. From historic temples to modern skyscrapers, this city is a must-visit for tech and culture lovers alike.

Paris, France

Known as the City of Light, Paris is a romantic paradise filled with world-class art, cuisine, and fashion. The Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum are just the beginning of its charm.

New York City, USA

NYC is a bustling metropolis with iconic landmarks like Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty. Its energy and diversity make it a global hub of culture and business.

Bali, Indonesia

Bali is an island paradise known for its breathtaking beaches, lush rice terraces, and spiritual retreats. It’s the perfect destination for relaxation and adventure alike.

Cape Town, South Africa

With stunning coastal views, Table Mountain, and a rich history, Cape Town is a gem in South Africa. Whether you're into nature, history, or food, Cape Town has something to offer.
